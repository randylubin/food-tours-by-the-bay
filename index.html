Our Tour

An incredible taste of North Beach.

We offer a two hour food tour of North Beach with five food tastings, some savory and some sweet. Along the way you will hear the history Gold Rush and of the Italian community that brings the neighborhood to life.

Possible bites:

  • Chocolate and fudge
  • Italian deli tasting
  • Mini-cannolis
  • Authenitic pizza

Alcatraz Pairing

Alcatraz + Food Tour is a perfect pairing!

Want to experience Alcatraz while in San Francisco? Learn about Alcatraz’s famous inmates on this island prison. Take the ferry from Pier 33 and spend a few hours wandering through the prison cells and learning about Al Capone, The Birdman, and “Machine Gun” Kelly.

Key Details:

  • Alcatraz packages are only available with 4 days or more advance notice.
  • Your Alcatraz ticket is for a morning departure (9am-noon, possible) and our tour is in the afternoon
  • Package includes a special Food Tours By the Bay gift
  • The retail price of a ticket to Alcatraz is $33.00 and our package includes tour guide gratuity for our tour
